About Us


Welcome to Daisy Delights, your premier destination for exquisite flowers and floral arrangements. Founded and developed by Bharti Jayprakash, Daisy Delights is a passion project that stems from our love for nature's most beautiful creations. At Daisy Delights, we believe that every occasion deserves the touch of nature's elegance. Whether it's a special celebration, a heartfelt gesture, or simply brightening someone's day, our carefully curated collection of flowers is designed to convey your emotions with beauty and grace.
Bharti Jayprakash, with her expertise in web development and keen eye for floral design and web design, came together to create an online flower shopping experience like no other. Our user-friendly interface, coupled with a wide selection of the freshest blooms, ensures that your journey with Daisy Delights is seamless and delightful. From vibrant bouquets to elegant arrangements, each Bloomify creation is a masterpiece crafted to bring joy and warmth to your moments. We source our flowers directly from trusted growers, guaranteeing quality and freshness with every delivery.
